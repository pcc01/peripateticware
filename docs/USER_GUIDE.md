## Getting Started

### System Requirements

**Web Application**
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- JavaScript enabled
- Cookies enabled

**Mobile App**
- iOS 12.0+ or Android 6.0+
- 50 MB free storage
- Location services (optional, for location-based activities)
- Push notifications (optional)

### Creating Your Account

#### For Teachers
1. Go to `https://peripateticware.example.com`
2. Click "Sign Up" → "Teacher Account"
3. Enter your name and email
4. Create a strong password (at least 8 characters)
5. Select your school/district
6. Verify your email
7. You're ready to go!

#### For Parents
1. Download the Peripateticware app from App Store or Play Store
2. Click "Create Account"
3. Enter your name and email
4. Create a password
5. Select your relationship to the student
6. Verify your email
7. Link your child's account (provide child's name or link code)

#### For Administrators
Contact your system administrator for login credentials

## For Teachers

### Dashboard Overview

When you log in, you'll see your **Teacher Dashboard** with:
- **Your Classes**: List of all your classes
- **Active Learners**: Current students and their status
- **Quick Stats**: Overview of class activity
- **Create Activity**: Button to start a new activity

### Creating a Learning Activity

#### Step 1: Plan Your Activity
1. Click "Create Activity"
2. Fill in basic information:
   - **Activity Name**: e.g., "Nature Walk - Spring Flowers"
   - **Learning Area**: Select from curriculum areas
   - **Duration**: How long the activity will take
   - **Location**: Where the activity takes place
   - **Description**: What students will learn

#### Step 2: Set Up Objectives
1. Select learning objectives from your curriculum
2. Specify what students should observe/discover
3. Add guiding questions for students
4. Set success criteria

#### Step 3: Configure Settings
- **Outdoor Location**: Set GPS coordinates or address
- **Real-time Monitoring**: Enable to see student submissions live
- **Evidence Types**: Allow photos, notes, drawings, audio
- **Collaboration**: Allow students to work together
- **Time Limit**: Set if you want a specific duration

#### Step 4: Review & Launch
1. Preview how activity appears to students
2. Make any adjustments
3. Click "Launch Activity"
4. Share link or code with students

### Managing Student Submissions

#### Viewing Evidence
1. Click on a student's name to see their submission
2. View all evidence (photos, notes, audio)
3. See the student's reflection
4. Check time spent on activity

#### Providing Feedback
1. Click "Add Feedback" on a submission
2. Write constructive comments
3. Optionally add a learning level:
   - Emerging
   - Developing
   - Proficient
   - Advanced
4. Students see feedback immediately

#### Creating Competency Records
1. After reviewing submissions, click "Record Evidence"
2. Select which competencies were demonstrated
3. Add notes about what you observed
4. This contributes to student's overall profile

### Monitoring Real-Time Activity

#### Live View
- Click "Live Monitor" during an active activity
- See all students' locations on map
- View submissions as they come in
- Send notifications to all students

#### Filtering & Searching
- Filter by learning area
- Filter by date range
- Search for specific activities
- Sort by completion status

### Managing Your Classes

#### Adding Students
1. Go to "Classes" → Your class name
2. Click "Add Student"
3. Choose: Create new link, use existing code, or invite by email
4. Student accepts invitation

#### Organizing Students into Groups
1. Click "Manage Groups"
2. Click "Create Group"
3. Select students to add
4. Save group
5. Use groups for collaborative activities

#### Tracking Progress
1. Click "Class Progress"
2. View each student's:
   - Activities completed
   - Evidence collected
   - Competencies demonstrated
   - Learning trajectory

## For Parents

### Mobile App Features

#### Dashboard
Your personal dashboard shows:
- **Your Child**: Currently selected child
- **Recent Activities**: What your child has been doing
- **Progress Overview**: Learning areas and completion
- **Achievements**: Badges and milestones
- **Messages**: From teacher and school

#### Viewing Child's Progress

1. **Select Child**: Tap child's name at top
2. **See Activity History**:
   - All activities your child participated in
   - Photos and notes submitted
   - Teacher feedback
   - Competencies developed

3. **Learning Areas Breakdown**:
   - Visual breakdown by subject
   - Progress circle showing completion
   - Time spent on each area
   - Trends over time

#### Notification Preferences

1. Open **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Choose what you want to know:
   - ✓ Activity completed
   - ✓ Achievements unlocked
   - ✓ Teacher messages
   - ✓ Concerns flagged
   - ✓ Weekly summary
3. Choose notification method (push, email, text)

#### Understanding Feedback

**Teacher Feedback Levels**
- 🌱 **Emerging**: Student is beginning to show understanding
- 🌿 **Developing**: Student is building skills
- 🌳 **Proficient**: Student demonstrates expected level
- 🌲 **Advanced**: Student goes beyond expectations

**What This Means**
- Feedback shows what your child did well
- Suggestions for how to extend learning at home
- Links to resources for further exploration

### Offline Features

The app works even without internet:
- View past activity submissions
- Read teacher feedback
- Write notes (syncs when online)
- See your child's progress

When you reconnect:
- All changes sync automatically
- No data is lost

## Common Tasks

### Task: Create a Walking Field Trip Activity

**Time**: 15 minutes

1. **Plan**: Decide on route, learning objectives, evidence to collect
2. **Create Activity**:
   - Name: "Walking Tour of Local Community"
   - Learning Area: "Social Studies"
   - Duration: 60 minutes
   - Location: Set GPS points for start and end
3. **Set Objectives**:
   - Students identify 5 community landmarks
   - Photograph and describe each
   - Reflect on how community helps people
4. **Configure**:
   - Enable real-time monitoring
   - Allow photo and note submissions
   - Set 60-minute time limit
5. **Launch**:
   - Share with class
   - Monitor submissions live
   - Provide real-time feedback via app

### Task: Assess Competency from Evidence

**Time**: 10 minutes per student

1. **Open Submission**: Click on student's activity
2. **Review Evidence**: Photos, notes, reflections
3. **Assess**:
   - Click "Record Competency"
   - Select competencies demonstrated
   - Rate level (emerging to advanced)
   - Add specific observations
4. **Document**: System creates record for portfolio
5. **Feedback**: Write constructive comment for student

### Task: Send Weekly Progress Update to Parents

**Time**: 20 minutes

1. **Go to Reports** → **Parent Communications**
2. **Select Class**: Choose your class
3. **Generate Summary**:
   - Week date range
   - Activities completed
   - Key learning moments
   - Competencies developed
4. **Add Personal Notes**: Comments for parents
5. **Send**: Email to all parents with weekly summary

### Task: Prepare for Parent-Teacher Conference

**Time**: 30 minutes

1. **Generate Student Report**:
   - Student name and date range
   - Activities participated in
   - Evidence collected
   - Competencies developed
   - Progress over time
2. **Analyze**:
   - Strengths and growth areas
   - Recommendations for home
   - Next learning targets
3. **Export**: Create PDF for conference
4. **Print or Share**: Give to parent during meeting

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### "I can't log in"
**Solution**:
1. Check your email/username spelling
2. Click "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Check email for verification link
4. Clear browser cache and try again
5. Try different browser if issue persists

#### "Activity won't load"
**Solution**:
1. Check internet connection
2. Refresh the page (Cmd+R or Ctrl+R)
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try on different device
5. Contact support if issue persists

#### "Can't submit evidence"
**Solution**:
1. Check file size (max 10 MB per file)
2. Ensure stable internet connection
3. Try uploading one file at a time
4. Try different file format
5. On mobile: Check camera/photo permissions

#### "Progress not updating"
**Solution**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Log out and log back in
3. Wait a few minutes (may be processing)
4. Check internet connection
5. Try on different device

#### "Not receiving notifications"
**Solution**:
1. Go to Settings → Notifications
2. Ensure notifications are enabled
3. Check permission for app/browser
4. Add app to "Do Not Disturb" exceptions
5. Restart the app

### Performance Issues

#### App is slow
1. Clear app cache:
   - iPhone: Settings → App → Peripateticware → Storage
   - Android: Settings → Apps → Peripateticware → Storage
2. Close other apps
3. Restart device
4. Update to latest app version
5. Check internet connection

#### Photos aren't uploading
1. Check photo file size (compress if needed)
2. Ensure good internet connection
3. Try uploading one photo at a time
4. Check phone storage space
5. Restart app

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. We use:
- Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
- Secure token-based authentication
- Database encryption at rest
- Regular security audits
- FERPA/GDPR compliance

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Yes. Mobile app works offline:
- View past activities
- Read feedback
- Write notes
- Syncs automatically when online

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: We support:
- Chrome (latest 2 versions)
- Firefox (latest 2 versions)
- Safari (latest 2 versions)
- Edge (latest 2 versions)

### For Teachers

**Q: How do I delete an activity?**
A: Go to Activities → Select Activity → Click ⋯ Menu → Archive Activity. (You can restore it later if needed.)

**Q: Can students work on activities together?**
A: Yes. When creating an activity, enable "Collaboration." Students can see each other's submissions and contribute together.

**Q: How do I make activities happen at specific times?**
A: You can schedule when students can access activities. Set "Start Time" and "End Time" when creating activity.

**Q: Can I use this for indoor activities?**
A: Yes! Location is optional. You can create activities for classrooms, gyms, libraries, etc.

**Q: How do I customize the curriculum?**
A: Contact your administrator. Curriculum areas can be aligned with your standards (Common Core, IB, local standards, etc.).

### For Parents

**Q: How often will I hear from my child's teacher?**
A: That's up to the teacher. You control notification frequency in Settings. Teachers may also send messages.

**Q: Can I share my child's progress?**
A: Yes. Click "Share" on your child's profile to send a summary via email. You can also print reports.

**Q: What if I'm concerned about something I see?**
A: Contact the teacher directly through the app or email. You can also call the school.

**Q: Can I download my child's data?**
A: Yes. Go to Settings → Privacy → Download My Data. You'll get all your child's activity records, evidence, and feedback.

### For Administrators

**Q: How do I migrate from another system?**
A: Contact our support team. We can help with:
- Data import from CSV or LMS
- User account migration
- Historical data transfer
- Curriculum alignment

**Q: Can I integrate with my LMS?**
A: Yes. We support:
- Canvas
- Google Classroom
- Blackboard
- Schoology
- Custom integrations available

**Q: How do I backup my data?**
A: Automatic daily backups are included. You can also manually export data from Settings → Data Management.

**Q: What's your uptime guarantee?**
A: We maintain 99.9% uptime. Status page: status.peripateticware.example.com

**Q: Can I white-label this system?**
A: Yes. Enterprise accounts can customize:
- Branding (logos, colors)
- Custom domain
- White-label reports
- Contact our sales team
